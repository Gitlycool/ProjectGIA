Building a Bright Future: Sunnyvale Mayor's Address at Fremont High's GIA Club


Date: 05/17/2023
Time: 12:45pm - 1:35pm
Location: 575 W Fremont Ave, Sunnyvale, CA 94087
Room #: 153

The city of Sunnyvale is a thriving hub of innovation, diversity, and community spirit. As we embark on a new chapter in our city's history, it is crucial to gather as a united community to discuss the challenges we face and the opportunities that lie ahead. To celebrate this spirit of collaboration, the Fremont High GIA Club proudly presents the Sunnyvale Mayor's Address.

This engaging event will provide an invaluable platform for our visionary Mayor to share his/her insights on the pressing issues that impact our community. From addressing concerns related to sustainability, infrastructure development, and public services to the need for enhanced education and employment opportunities, the Mayor will delve into the strategies and plans designed to tackle these challenges head-on.

The Fremont High GIA Club, a group of passionate students dedicated to raising awareness about global issues, is honored to host this event. They firmly believe that engaging with local leaders and fostering dialogue is key to creating a brighter future for our city and beyond.

During the Mayor's address, attendees will have the opportunity to gain unique perspectives on Sunnyvale's future trajectory, witness the unveiling of upcoming projects, and engage in a thought-provoking Q&A session. This interactive dialogue will encourage active participation from students, ensuring that every youth voice is heard and considered in shaping the city's future.

Join us for an evening of inspiration, collaboration, and shared aspirations at Fremont High School. Don't miss this chance to connect with like-minded individuals, learn about Sunnyvale's strategic plans, and contribute to the ongoing development of our vibrant community.

Please come to Fremont High School Rm. 153 on the event date and everyone in invited. Together, let's build a brighter future for Sunnyvale!

Note: Please ensure that you check for any updates or changes in the event details closer to the event date.


Boba/Chips Fundraiser for Turkey-Syria Earthquakes


Date: 03/25/2023
Time: 3:30pm - 5:00pm
Location: 575 W Fremont Ave, Sunnyvale, CA 94087

Come and join us for a heartwarming event as we host a Boba/Chips Fundraiser in support of the victims of the recent Turkey-Syria earthquakes. This event aims to raise funds and provide relief for those affected by the devastating earthquakes that have shaken the region.

Indulge in the delightful combination of delicious boba drinks and crunchy chips while making a difference in the lives of people who are in dire need of assistance. Our dedicated team has curated a wide selection of boba flavors and an assortment of chips to satisfy your taste buds.

As you sip on your favorite boba drink and savor the scrumptious chips, take pride in knowing that every purchase you make contributes directly to the humanitarian efforts in Turkey and Syria. All proceeds from the event will go towards providing emergency aid, shelter, medical supplies, and support to those affected by the earthquakes.

Save the date for 03/25/2023 and invite your friends, family, and neighbors to this important fundraising event. Together, let's extend our compassion beyond borders and provide hope and assistance to those affected by the Turkey-Syria earthquakes. Your presence and contribution will make a meaningful difference in the lives of those who need it most.

Join us for an afternoon of boba, chips, and giving back. Together, we can rebuild lives and bring hope to communities impacted by tragedy.

Clothing Drive Event


Date: 12/12/2022 - 12/16/2022
Time: 3:30pm - 5:00pm
Location: 575 W Fremont Ave, Sunnyvale, CA 94087
Room #: 86

Join us for a meaningful event as the Fremont High GIA Club hosts a Clothing Drive to support individuals and families in need. This initiative aims to collect gently used clothing items and make a positive impact in our community.

We invite you to go through your closets and donate any clean, gently used clothing that you no longer need. From shirts, pants, and dresses to jackets, shoes, and accessories, every contribution can make a difference in someone's life.

The Clothing Drive will provide an opportunity for you to give back and help those who may be facing financial hardships or other challenges. Your donated clothing will be distributed to local shelters, community organizations, and individuals who are in need of assistance.

By participating in this event, you'll not only declutter your wardrobe but also contribute to a more sustainable and compassionate society. Your act of generosity can bring warmth, dignity, and a renewed sense of hope to those less fortunate.

Join us from 12/12/2022 to 12/16/2022 at Fremont High School Rm. 86 to drop off your donated clothing items. Our dedicated volunteers will be on hand to receive your donations and provide any necessary assistance. Additionally, we will have information available about local organizations and initiatives that support those in need, allowing you to learn more about ongoing efforts in our community.

Let's come together as a community to support one another and make a positive impact. Spread the word, invite your friends, family, and neighbors to participate in this Clothing Drive hosted by the GIA Club. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of individuals and families who are in need of clothing and a helping hand.

We look forward to your support and generosity. Thank you for being a part of this meaningful event.
